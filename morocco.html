The Kingdom Of Morocco

The official name of Morocco in Arabic is 'Al Mamlaka al-Maghrebiya' meaning the Kingdom of Morocco. In Arabic Morocco is called 'Al Maghrib' meaning 'Land of Sunset'. Morocco is a country situated in North Africa, just south of Europe. Where Spain is only 8.6 miles (14 Km) away from the Moroccan border.

Life in Morocco

Morocco is food. Morocco is culture. Morocco is the smell of petrol in the air. Morocco is the old lady decorating the hand of a young girl with henna. Morocco is a sunset behind the palm trees. Morocco is history. Morocco is a unique country.

Religion & Language

The official language in Morocco is Arabic, but people speak the moroccan arabic dialect "Darija". The moroccan people can speak also french, spanish, and english.
Morocco is an Islamic country, you can find a lot of historical mosques that gave access to everyone to enter.

Food

In Morocco, meals are particularly important moments. Very friendly, they are almost rituals. Eating with hands is a true tradition. Some traditional meals:


  • Tagines
  • Moroccan Tea
  • Couscous
