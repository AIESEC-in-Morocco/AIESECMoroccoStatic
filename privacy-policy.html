Cookies can be classified as "persistent" or "session". While the former remain on the PC or mobile device despite being disconnected, the latter are deleted as soon as you close web browsing.

Our main activity: The Association is non-profit and pursues as its supreme objective the achievement of peace and the full development of human potential, through:

  • the organization and promotion of international exchange programs aimed at the socio-cultural integration of foreign university students and recent graduates in Morocco and Moroccans abroad, which favor their personal and professional growth in a context different from that of origin;
  • the development and promotion of programs that allow the acquisition and enhancement of skills and competences in young university students and recent graduates thanks to teamwork experiences and their commitment to projects in which they hold positions of responsibility;
  • the promotion of information, awareness and training activities in collaboration with universities, public and private bodies and other organizations around issues of global importance and common interest, related to the world of young people and the socio-economic and entrepreneurial development of our country;
  • the development of cooperation initiatives with other peoples in order to foster and improve cultural understanding and the exchange of knowledge and experience between the different realities involved;
  • assistance and promotion of entrepreneurial activities, the design and delivery of training and alternance courses;
  • the organization of conferences and events aimed at providing its members with training and education for the achievement of the purposes indicated above.
